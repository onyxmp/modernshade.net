IP Addresses and Cookies

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our website. Your IP address is also used to gather broad demographic information..

Cookies are small pieces of text stored on your computer (not our site) that help us know which browser you’re using and where you’ve been on our site. Most cookies are “session cookies” which means they’re automatically deleted at the end of each session. A cookie itself does not have the ability to automatically collect personal information about you. A cookie can store certain information that identifies your computer to us so that you don’t have to re-enter information as frequently when you use our site.
